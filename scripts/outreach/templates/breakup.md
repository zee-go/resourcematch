# Breakup (Touch 4 — Day 14)
# 40-60 words

## Structure
1. Acknowledge they're busy (genuine, not passive-aggressive)
2. Leave the door open with a no-pressure statement
3. Optional: mention one last specific value point

## Example tone
"I know you're busy, so I'll leave it here. If you ever need pre-vetted
senior accounting talent from the Philippines, resourcematch.ph is always there.
Happy to help whenever the timing is right."

## Do NOT
- Be guilt-trippy ("This is my last email...")
- Use dramatic "breakup" language
- Add urgency or fake scarcity

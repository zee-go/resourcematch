# Cold Intro (Touch 1 — Day 0)
# 80-120 words

## Structure
1. Opening: Reference a specific signal about their company (job posting, growth, tech stack)
2. Bridge: Connect their need to what ResourceMatch offers
3. Proof: Name 1-2 specific candidates with years of experience and standout skill
4. CTA: "Want me to send their profiles?" or "Open to a quick look?"

## Example tone
"Noticed [company] is hiring a [role] — we have [Name], an 8-year [skill] specialist
who just passed our 4-layer AI vetting with a 92/100 score. She's handled [relevant task]
for companies like yours. Want me to send you her full profile?"

## Do NOT
- Start with "I hope this email finds you well"
- Use generic value props without specifics
- Mention pricing

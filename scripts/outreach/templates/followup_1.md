## Structure
1. Short reference to previous email (don't repeat it)
2. Add a new proof point: a platform stat, a client result, or a relevant case study
3. Reinforce the CTA

- "Our clients typically save 60-70% compared to US-based hires for the same experience level"
- "[Candidate name]'s last project: managed $2M in accounts receivable for a US e-commerce brand"

## Do NOT
- Re-explain ResourceMatch from scratch
- Be pushy or guilt-trip about not replying

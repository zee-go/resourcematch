# Follow-up 2 (Touch 3 — Day 7)
# 60-80 words

## Structure
1. Try a completely different angle from Touches 1-2
2. Options: share a relevant blog post, mention a new candidate, address a common objection
3. Keep it casual and low-pressure

## Angle ideas
- Link to a relevant ResourceMatch blog post (from resourcematch.ph/blog)
- Mention a different candidate who just completed vetting
- Address the "is remote talent reliable?" objection with specific vetting details
- Reference industry trends in remote hiring

## Do NOT
- Sound frustrated that they haven't replied
- Use "just following up" or "bumping this to the top"

## Time Zone: How It Works for US Companies

Manila is 12 to 16 hours ahead of US time zones depending on the coast and season. That gap is a feature if you structure it correctly. Many US companies run a night-cycle finance operation: their Filipino Finance Manager completes the prior day's close, reconciliations, and reporting overnight, so US leadership arrives to completed work each morning.

For roles requiring real-time overlap, Filipino professionals regularly work adjusted hours covering US Eastern morning windows (roughly 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM EST, which is 9:00 PM to 1:00 AM Manila time).

### Are Filipino offshore contractors subject to Australian superannuation?

No. Superannuation obligations under Australian law apply to employees and, in some cases, contractors working substantially for one business under Australian arrangements. Offshore contractors based in the Philippines and engaged directly as independent contractors are generally outside the scope of Australian super requirements. You should confirm your specific engagement structure with your Australian tax adviser, but this is a consistent cost saving for businesses hiring offshore.

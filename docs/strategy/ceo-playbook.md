> Last updated: 2026-03-09
> Status: Pre-revenue, MVP complete, deployed to GCP Cloud Run

---

## Executive Summary

ResourceMatch is a B2B marketplace connecting international companies with AI-vetted senior Filipino professionals (5-10+ years experience). The platform has a production-ready MVP with 50 API routes, 19 Prisma models, Stripe payments, a 4-layer AI vetting pipeline, and an automated SEO content agent — but zero revenue.

**Mission:** Make hiring senior Filipino professionals as easy and trustworthy as buying from Amazon.

**Target:** $20K+/mo revenue by month 12, $50K/mo by month 18.

**Two active verticals:** Finance & Accounting + Operations Management.

**Core differentiator:** 4-layer AI vetting pipeline + senior-only talent pool + $25/unlock pricing (vs. $2,000-7,500 traditional staffing fees).

## Funnel Strategy

### Two Parallel Funnels

```
FUNNEL A: BROWSE & UNLOCK (Profile Discovery)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
Awareness → Browse Profiles → Signup → Free Unlock (2x) → Paid Unlock → Subscription
  SEO          5-8% of         40-60%      15-20%          10% of
  Ads          visitors        activate    convert          active
  LinkedIn
  Cold email

FUNNEL B: FREE JOB POSTING (Recurring Engagement) ← STRONGEST DEMAND LEVER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Post Job → Candidates Apply → See Vetted Applicants → Unlock Top Match → Repeat
  $0           platform          AI match score            $25/unlock
  zero         delivers          shown, contact            natural
  friction     candidates        info locked               paywall
```

### Why Free Job Posting Is the #1 Demand Driver

1. **Zero friction entry** — companies get immediate value (applicants) without paying
2. **Recurring engagement** — companies return to check applications daily/weekly
3. **Natural paywall** — vetted applicants with locked contact info → "Unlock for $25"
4. **Content flywheel** — job listings attract candidates who browse jobs
5. **Ongoing monetization surface** — unlike 2 free unlocks (one-time), job posting creates continuous conversion opportunities

### Freemium Strategy (2 Free Unlocks)

- Hook for profile browsers who haven't posted a job yet
- Risk with small candidate pool (<50): companies may not find a match and churn
- Mitigation: AI matching suggests best-fit candidates for the free unlocks
- Surface highest-vetting-score candidates to maximize wow factor
- Track `freeUnlocksUsed` → if both used but no purchase, trigger email drip

### Target Conversion Rates

| Stage | Target | Benchmark |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| Visitor → Signup | 5-8% | SaaS avg 2-5% |
| Signup → Free Unlock Used | 40-60% | Product-led avg 30-50% |
| Free User → First Paid Unlock | 15-20% | Freemium avg 2-5% (ours higher due to high intent) |
| Job Poster → First Paid Unlock | 25-35% | Higher because qualified applicants create urgency |
| First Purchase → Repeat (90 days) | 60%+ | Top marketplaces 50-70% |
| Active Company → Subscription | 10-15% | SaaS avg 5-15% |

## Demand Side Acquisition (Companies)

### Tier 1 — Founder-Led (Months 1-3, $0 cost)

**This is where all demand comes from initially. No shortcuts.**

- LinkedIn personal brand: 2 posts/day on hiring Filipino talent, outsourcing tips, behind-the-scenes building ResourceMatch
- Direct outreach: 25 decision-makers/week via LinkedIn DM (personalized, reference their company)
- Warm intros from personal network
- Attend 2 virtual events/month (indie hacker, SMB, outsourcing communities)

**Target ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):**
- US/AU/UK SMBs, 10-50 employees, $1-10M revenue
- Already outsourcing OR actively exploring (search for "virtual assistant" or "outsource" in their posts)
- Key roles: bookkeeper, financial analyst, accountant, inventory/ops manager, Shopify specialist
- Decision maker: founder, COO, or head of finance

### Tier 2 — Content & SEO (Months 2-6, $0-500/mo)

- **Kelly SEO agent**: 2 blog posts/week (already running)
- **Target keywords** (transactional intent):
  - "hire Filipino accountant"
  - "outsource bookkeeping Philippines"
  - "senior Filipino VA"
  - "Filipino operations manager"
  - "remote Filipino CPA"
- **Newsletter**: weekly talent spotlight + industry data (Beehiiv or Substack, free tier)
- **YouTube**: monthly "Hiring in the Philippines" video (case studies, tips, market data)

### Tier 3 — Outbound Sales (Months 3-6, $1.5-2K/mo)

- **Cold email**: 500 prospects/week via Apollo.io
- **Email templates** (3-email sequence):
  1. Problem-aware: "Hiring a senior accountant costs $75K+ in the US. Same role in PH: $18-25K."
  2. Solution-aware: "We AI-vet every candidate across 4 layers. See profiles for $25."
  3. Social proof: "Here's how [Beta Client] hired their financial controller through ResourceMatch."
- **SDR hire**: Filipino SDR at $1.5-2K/mo for qualification calls and demo scheduling

### Tier 4 — Paid & Partnerships (Months 4-6+, $500-3K/mo)

- **LinkedIn Ads**: $500-1K/mo targeting HR/ops decision-makers at SMBs
- **Google Ads**: $500-1K/mo on transactional keywords ("hire Filipino accountant")
- **Partner referrals**: bookkeeping firms, fractional CFOs, Shopify agencies, e-commerce consultants
- **Community sponsorships**: Indie Hackers, SaaS communities, accounting forums

## Decision Framework

When facing strategic decisions, apply these principles in order:

1. **Revenue before features** — if it doesn't drive revenue in the next 90 days, deprioritize
2. **Supply quality over quantity** — 30 great candidates > 100 mediocre ones
3. **Founder-led sales before paid acquisition** — learn the pitch, objections, and ICP firsthand
4. **One channel mastered before adding another** — going wide too early wastes money
5. **80/20 rule** — 20% of activities will drive 80% of results. Identify and double down.
6. **Placement success is the only real validation** — signups, traffic, likes don't matter without placements
7. **Candidate experience matters** — if candidates hate the vetting process, supply dries up
